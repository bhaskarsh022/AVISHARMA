Speed Ramping

Speed ramping is a technique used to adjust the speed of your video footage in post-production. This technique allows you to create dramatic effects by speeding up or slowing down your video at specific points. It's commonly used in action scenes or to emphasize particular moments.

Colour Grading

Colour grading is the process of enhancing the color, contrast, and overall look of your video footage. This step is crucial for setting the mood and tone of your video. Professional color grading can transform ordinary footage into a cinematic masterpiece.

Motion Tracking

Motion tracking allows you to track the movement of objects in your video and add effects or layers to follow that movement. This technique is widely used in visual effects to integrate elements seamlessly into live-action footage.
